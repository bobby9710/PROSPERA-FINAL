# Research Brief: Prospera Development Squad

## Objective
Establish a high-performance development squad to evolve the Prospera platform with a focus on premium UI/UX (Stitch), dark/light mode persistence, and financial intelligence features.

## Key Findings
- **Fintech Metrics:** Success depends on balancing customer acquisition with deep retention/churn analysis. "Stickiness" (DAU/MAU) and "Time to First Transaction" are critical.
- **UI/UX 2024:** Dark mode requires semantic palettes (avoiding #000). Information hierarchy must be minimalist to avoid cognitive load in financial dashboards.
- **Tech Stack:** React + Vite + Tailwind is the industry standard for performance. Supabase provides the necessary real-time backend and auth.
- **QA Strategy:** Shift-left testing with Vitest for logic and Playwright for real-browser visual regression is essential for design fidelity.
